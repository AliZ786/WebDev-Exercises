The history of the NBA

The game of basketball as it is known today was created by Dr. James Naismith in December 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts, to condition young athletes during cold months. The objective of the game was to throw the basketball into the fruit baskets nailed to the lower railing of the gym balcony.

The game has since evolved however, and has become the biggest international sport due to the emergence of the NBA, the National BasketBall Association.

Some fun facts about the NBA

  • One time NBA player Manute Bol killed a lion with a spear when he was fifteen years old in Africa.
  • Wilt Chamberlain scored 100 points, the most ever, in a single game.
  • Micheal Jordan, arguably the greatest player of all time, was drafted third after Hakeem Olajuwon and Sam Bowie
